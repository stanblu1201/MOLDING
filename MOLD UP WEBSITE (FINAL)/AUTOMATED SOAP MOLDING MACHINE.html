MACHINE INTERFACE

 

A machine interface is the point of interaction between a user and a machine, enabling control, monitoring, and data input/output. It includes displays, buttons, touchscreens, and other controls that facilitate user operation and feedback.

MOLDER

 

Molders are machines used in manufacturing to shape or form materials, typically plastics or metals, by injecting them into a mold where they cool and harden into the desired shape. Common types include injection molders and blow molders.

EJECTOR

 

An ejector is a device used in molding processes to push the finished part out of the mold cavity. It typically operates via mechanical or hydraulic means to ensure the part is released smoothly and without damage after the molding cycle is complete.

LIMIT SWITCH

 

A limit switch is an electromechanical device that detects the presence or absence of an object or the position of a part within a machine. It opens or closes an electrical circuit when a predetermined limit is reached, helping control and automate machinery operations.
